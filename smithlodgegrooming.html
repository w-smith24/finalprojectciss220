Grooming done the way you want

We have packages that fit your needs. And if they don't, we offer each service by itself at a reasonable price. From bathing to haircuts, we are here to groom your dog while you do anything you want to do. We'll call you when we are finished and return your dog to you fresh and clean. Prices are as follows:
